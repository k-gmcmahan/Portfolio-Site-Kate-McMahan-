About

My Story

I’m a UX Designer based in the greater Philadelphia area with an insatiable passion for learning. This passion stems from a combination of my teaching background and living in many different cultures. If there is anything important that I’ve taken away from my past experiences, it is that the cycle of learning never stops—no matter how old you are. I do my best to instill this approach in everything I design. Finding new angles to look at problems, constantly asking questions, and making improvements along the way. The only thing more powerful than learning alone, however, is learning together. Whether it is between companies and clients or development and design teams. That is where I believe innovation and change truly begins. So, as I like to say to my students, let’s get to it!

“..But who are you, really?” Fair enough! Here are some random facts:

  • I love trying weird flavor combinations.
  • You shouldn’t play against me in any game involving Disney trivia.
  • Pinterest DIY crafts are my guilty pleasure.
  • I am quietly waiting for a coffee IV-drip to be invented.
  • If we get to know each other better, I will inevitably show you lots of pictures of my dog.
